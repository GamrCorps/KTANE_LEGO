Structure Generation Algorithm

>1. Choose 10 random bricks (with weighted randomness factor)
>2. Choose 1 of those and place it down in the structure.
>3. Foreach other brick:
>  a. Choose which already-placed brick to attach it to.
>  b. Choose whether to attach to the top or bottom.
>  c. Choose which stud on that face to attach to.
>  d. Choose which direction to place brick.
>  e. Record connection(s).
>  [At any point, if cannot place piece there, move back a step]
4. Go through each connection. Figure out the top-down view of the instruction.
5. Randomize order of the instruction pages.
6. Make it look nice on module.
